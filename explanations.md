## How Does Aura Work?

### The Simple Version

You type a question or statement, press send, and Aura responds with helpful, encouraging advice focused on sustainability. It's really that simple!

You can also upload pictures and ask Aura about them. For example, you could take a photo of your lunch and ask, "How sustainable is this meal?" or snap a picture of a product label and ask if it's eco-friendly.

### The Technical Version (For the Curious)

Aura is built like a modern website application. Here's what happens behind the scenes:

1. **Your Message**: When you type something and hit send, your message is captured by the application.

2. **The Brain**: Aura uses Google's Gemini AI (a powerful artificial intelligence system) as its "brain." This AI has been specially instructed to think about everything through a sustainability lens.

3. **Memory**: Just like in a real conversation, Aura remembers what you've talked about earlier in the chat (up to the last 50 messages). This helps it give you relevant, context-aware responses.

4. **The Response**: The AI processes your message, thinks about sustainability angles, and crafts a helpful response that's practical and encouraging.

5. **Display**: The response appears in your chat window, formatted nicely with bullet points, bold text, and organized sections to make it easy to read.

## Common Questions About Aura

### Does Aura judge me?
Absolutely not! Aura is designed to be encouraging and supportive, never judgmental.

### Is the advice scientifically accurate?
Aura is powered by advanced AI trained on vast amounts of information, including scientific data about sustainability. While it strives for accuracy, always verify critical information from authoritative sources for important decisions.

### Can I use Aura offline?
Currently, Aura needs an internet connection to work because it communicates with Google's AI service.

### Does Aura remember everything I say?
Aura remembers your conversation history during your current chat session (up to the last 50 messages). This helps maintain context. When you clear the chat or close the application, this memory is reset.

### Is Aura available in other languages?
The current version primarily communicates in English, but it can understand and respond in multiple languages depending on your input.

### Can I trust the product recommendations?
Aura provides general guidance and suggestions based on sustainability principles. It doesn't promote specific brands (unless as examples) and isn't paid to recommend products.

### Key Components

**ChatInterface**: The heart of the application. Manages the conversation state, handles sending messages, receiving responses, and displaying the chat.

**API Configuration**: Located in `src/config/api.ts`, this contains the Google AI API key, endpoint URL, and the system prompt that defines Aura's personality and capabilities.

**Message Management**: The app maintains two message arrays:
- `messages`: All messages displayed in the UI
- `conversationHistory`: Recent context sent to the AI (limited to 50 messages for efficiency)

### The AI System Prompt

The system prompt is Aura's "personality instruction manual." It tells the AI:
- Who it is (Aura, the Sustainability Copilot)
- How to behave (encouraging, non-judgmental, knowledgeable)
- What to focus on (sustainability in all topics)
- How to format responses (markdown with bullets, bold, italics)
- The tone to use (friendly, practical, empowering)

### Google Gemini Integration

Aura uses Google's Gemini AI model (specifically, the flash variant for fast responses). The integration:
1. Formats your message and conversation history
2. Sends it to Google's API
3. Receives the AI-generated response
4. Displays it in the chat interface

The AI can also process images, allowing for visual analysis of products, situations, or anything else you want to ask about.

### Safety Settings

Aura includes content safety filters that block harmful, hateful, or inappropriate content, ensuring a safe, positive experience.
